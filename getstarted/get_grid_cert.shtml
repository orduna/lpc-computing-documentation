Getting Grid Certificate

For directions on how to get an account at CERN and registering as a member of CMS can be found on Section 1.2 (Get an Account) of the CMS twiki.

Next, you'll need to get a personal certificate signed by a CMS-registered Certificate Authority (CA). Please follow these procedures to obtain one. This may take a day or two; we are currently working to streamline the process.

Next, you'll need to register yourself as a member of the CMS Virtual Organization (VO) using your personal certificate.
